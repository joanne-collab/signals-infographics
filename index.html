Organizational and Behavioral Drivers of Mental Fatigue

• Productivity peaks when using two to three AI tools simultaneously, but scores dip significantly when a fourth tool is added.

• High AI oversight—the direct monitoring of agents—is the most taxing engagement, predicting 19% greater information overload.

• Managerial support is a critical buffer: workers whose managers answer AI questions report 15% lower mental fatigue scores.

• The 'AI orphan tax' occurs when employees are expected to figure out tools on their own, resulting in measurably higher fatigue.

• Corporate messaging regarding AI-boosted efficiency often signals unintended 'work intensification' to employees.
