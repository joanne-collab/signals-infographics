Claude Research Summary:
The shift towards AI-driven automation in small businesses is accelerating. 
Key findings:
- 75% of small business owners plan to implement AI by 2025.
- Cost savings of up to 30% are expected in the first year.
- Customer service and data entry are the primary targets for automation.

Strategic Steps:
1. Audit current manual processes.
2. Identify low-hanging fruit (repetitive tasks).
3. Pilot AI tools in one department.
4. Scale based on ROI.

Comparison: Manual vs AI
Manual: High labor costs, human error, slow turnaround.
AI: Lower long-term costs, high precision, 24/7 operation.

Source: AI for SMBs Report, 2024.
